Electrification Plan

Fleet Transition Roadmap

Our structured framework guarantees a seamless transition from diesel or gasoline to electric fleet operations.

Step 1

Feasibility & Audit

We assess depot power limits, daily fleet mileage, and utility grids to design a custom charging configuration.

Step 2

Infrastructure Design

Engineering site schematics, choosing AC vs DC hardware, and submitting building permits to local agencies.

Step 3

Installation & Connect

Deploying chargers, grid transformers, and sub-panels safely with our network of certified electrical contractors.

Step 4

Software Activation

Connecting chargers to the EV Fleet cloud dashboard, configuring dynamic load limits and user access RFID cards.

Step 5

Optimization & ESG

Analyzing session logs to trim peak energy tariffs, exporting carbon credit offsets, and scaling charging terminals.
