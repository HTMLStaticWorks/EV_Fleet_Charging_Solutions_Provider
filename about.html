Transitioning Clean Transit

The EV Fleet Story

Founded in 2022, EV Fleet was established to accelerate the transition of commercial transportation to zero-emission technology. We envisioned an all-in-one system where premium charging hardware meets smart cloud monitoring and robust utility load management.

Whether you are a logistics operator managing 20 local delivery vans or a municipality deploying 100 electric buses, EV Fleet provides the ideal combination of grid audit expertise, OCPP hardware, and software tracking to guarantee a smooth transition.

Explore Solutions

Our Mission

To provide smart grid connectivity and robust infrastructure solutions that empower fleets to transition to electric power efficiently. We deliver hardware sourcing, load management software, and carbon reporting to systematically cut operational costs.

Our Vision

To define the future of clean commercial transit. We envision a globally connected grid network of smart charging terminals that balance energy loads and support sustainable fleet operations everywhere.

Our Core Pillars

The values that guide every installer, engineer, and dispatcher inside EV Fleet.

Grid & Safety Integrity

Rigorous electrical safety compliance, certified cabinet housing, and continuous terminal fault checks to keep grid operations secure.

Tech-Driven Energy

Integrating OCPP charging terminals, real-time load management APIs, and dynamic dashboard reports to accelerate energy cost reduction.

Radical Scalability

Modular hub designs, support for standard connector types (CCS, NACS), and configurable portals tailored for depots of all sizes.

ESG Preservation

Partnering with municipal agencies for grid resiliency, greenhouse gas reduction telemetry, and carbon credit offsets.
